Informed Pregnancy

by Adherence To Antenatal and Postnatal Services

It's a thrill to follow along as your baby or child grows and see what's new every week. ATAPS is a Pregnancy, Baby, and Child Development Calendar.

Use ATAPS to find out what is going on with your baby during your pregnancy and what changes to expect during pregnancy.

Whom Its For?

ATAPS is open to everybody but more specically...

  • Expectant Mothers ( and Fathers ).
  • First-time mothers
  • People in Pre-pregnacy preparations
  • Mothers with New Borns
  • Anyone interested in meternal health
  • Medical Students

How It Works

See how you can get started right away

An expecting mother registers by sending REGISTER to our short code

The expecting mother receives questions to help identify the status of the pregnancy.

Once the expecting mother is registered the system sends weekly messages to keep the mother informed throughout the pregnancy.
